Get your web project done while creating up-and-coming developer talent.

For centuries, journeypeople have been incredibly important members of the economy. They're folks who are fully educated in a trade or craft, but not yet masters. After completing an apprenticeship, these journeypeople would travel from town to town, taking on different projects and gaining experience. Eventually, they would be invited to settle and become that town's master. And the cycle continued.

At JourneyMade, we're taking our own spin on the journeyperson tradition and applying it to web development. Under the guidance of Brenna O'Brien and Christina Truong (our resident masters) and after nine weeks of full-time training at HackerYou's front-end development immersive, our team members are ready and willing to work on your projects. One day they'll leave us to join another company full-time. And that's kind of the point.
